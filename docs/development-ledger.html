Final integrated deliverable

A scholarly research workspace where a user can ingest texts, read them, map stable locators, highlight and annotate passages, generate scholarly citations, organize annotations into research threads, search across recovered source material, and receive grounded answers only when citations can be tied back to source passages.

Workflow rule: Move sequentially. One active gate at a time. Do not advance until the active gate is implemented, audited, CI-verified, and closed. Revisit green gates only for bugs, regressions, or necessary refactors.
